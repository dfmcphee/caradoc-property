About

Caradoc Cart Services Ltd. was created in 2001 with the purpose of servicing the golf industry. In 2015 a property services division was formed when the company was relocated to Barry’s Bay Ontario.

Caradoc Property Services provides property maintenance services to the discerning property owner whether they own a residential, cottage, commercial, farm or special use property.

Residential Services

  • Snowplowing for residential homeowners, contracted or one-of,
  • Snowplowing for non-principal residence emergency services access, seasonal or year round use,
  • Snowplowing for retail, commercial or special use properties,
  • Seasonal landscaping and grounds maintenance, spring summer and fall
  • Summerizing and winterizing seasonal residences, get your property ready for opening or closing with partial or full service, i.e. plumbing prepared for use and/or plumbing drained and winter proofed, spring and fall tidying cleanup,
  • Docks/Boats in and/or out, with outside service contractors involved in boat service or wrapping,
  • Four seasons property maintenance, landscaping/ snowplowing
  • Flowerbed design and/or maintenance,
  • Tree pruning, shrub and hedge maintenance,
  • Minor repairs to properties or will refer to larger contractors if necessary,
  • Golf Cart sales and service to all makes
  • Property security checks, ad hoc or scheduled,
  • Area On-Call Emergency Property Services when you are unable to get there.
  • Insurance certificate provided upon request.
